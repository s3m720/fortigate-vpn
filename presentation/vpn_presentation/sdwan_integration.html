SD-WAN Integration with VPN

SD-WAN VPN Architecture

Key Components

FortiGate SD-WAN Hubs/Spokes: Devices terminating VPN tunnels and performing path selection.
Multiple WAN Links: Diverse internet connections (MPLS, broadband, LTE) forming the SD-WAN fabric.
IPsec VPN Tunnels: Secure tunnels established over various WAN links.
SD-WAN Zone: Logical grouping of WAN interfaces including VPN interfaces.

Integration Flow

VPN Tunnel Creation: Multiple IPsec VPN tunnels established over different WAN links.
SD-WAN Zone Configuration: VPN tunnel interfaces added as members to an SD-WAN zone.
Performance SLA Monitoring: SLAs configured to monitor quality of each VPN tunnel.
SD-WAN Rules for VPN Traffic: Rules created to steer traffic based on SLA metrics.
Dynamic Path Selection: FortiGate selects optimal VPN tunnel for each traffic flow.

Performance Metrics

Latency: Time delay for packets to travel from source to destination.
Jitter: Variation in packet delay, critical for real-time applications.
Packet Loss: Percentage of packets that fail to reach their destination.
