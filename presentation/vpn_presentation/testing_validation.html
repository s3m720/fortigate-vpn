Testing and Validation

Performance Metrics

SSL VPN Test Scenarios

Successful Connection: Verify remote user can establish SSL VPN connection and access internal resources.
Invalid Credentials: Verify users with invalid credentials are denied access.
Unauthorized User: Verify users not in authorized groups are denied access.
Access to Restricted Resources: Verify VPN users can only access permitted resources.
Disconnection and Reconnection: Verify stable connection and proper session termination.

IPsec VPN Test Scenarios

Bidirectional Connectivity: Verify ping between Head Office and Branch Office LANs.
Internal Resource Access: Verify access to web servers and file shares across sites.
Tunnel Status Monitoring: Verify tunnel remains up and stable with active SAs.
Tunnel Rekeying: Verify correct rekeying without traffic interruption.
Dead Peer Detection: Verify detection of unresponsive peers and tunnel teardown.

SD-WAN Validation

Zone and Member Status: Verify SD-WAN zone is active with VPN tunnels as members.
Performance SLA Monitoring: Verify SLAs are monitoring VPN tunnels with accurate metrics.
SD-WAN Rule Effectiveness: Verify traffic is steered according to configured rules.
Failover and Redundancy: Test failover by simulating WAN link or VPN tunnel failure.
